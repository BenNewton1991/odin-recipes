Lasagna

Lasagna is an itallian dish made up of lamb, beef, pasta sheets, tomatos

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • White sauce
  • Gravy
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Fry beef
  2. Add tomatos
  3. Put in baking tray
  4. Layer on white sauce and lasagna
  5. Oven for 30 mins
  6. Add cheese and over for 15 more minutes
